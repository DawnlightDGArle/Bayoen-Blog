Released on December 8th 2016 on the Nintendo 3DS, this 25th Anniversary Special Edition box originally retailed for the equivalent of $100USD and comes with Puyo Puyo Chronicle, a special 25th anniversary soundtrack, a 25th anniversary Drama CD and a 25th anniversary vocal track. The red puyo disc contains the original soundtrack for the game, the blue puyo disc contains the drama cd tracks and the green puyo disc contains the vocal tracks and additional background music.
