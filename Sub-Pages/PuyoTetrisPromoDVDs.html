Two promotional DVDs for Puyo Puyo Tetris. Released sometime between 2013 and 2014 as a preview for what Puyo Puyo Tetris could be advertised as, the discs contain footage showcasing gameplay as well as other features of the game including a showcase of the game's various game modes. Notably, the gameplay is sourced from both the Playstation 4 and Xbox One versions of the game.
