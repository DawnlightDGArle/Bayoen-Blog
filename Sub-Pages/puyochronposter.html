An advertising poster advertising the release of Nintendo 3DS title, Puyo Puyo Chronicle. Released as the 25th anniversary title for the Puyo Puyo series on December 8th, 2016, the game featured an RPG mode that integrated the mechanics of Puyo Puyo as well as including a party system, items, skills and other elements commonly found in most JRPG titles and having 6 dungeons to explore and conquer. The game also featured two new characters, protagonist Ally and main antagonist, Rafisol, as well as also having a series of unique side quests involving most of the cast including the two new characters.

The poster itself is unique in that it specifically chooses to advertise the game's "Anniversary Edition", which included 3 bonus discs containing OST from the game and a special 25th Anniversary Drama CD featuring Ally. The poster also features two screenshots, neither of which do a particularly great job of accurately representing Puyo Puyo Chronicle's main selling point, the RPG main story mode, as both screenshots only show dialouge between Arle and the Mayor of Grimp Town and Arle opening a chest in Warmly Volcano respectively as opposed to the actual in-game battle or skills systems.
