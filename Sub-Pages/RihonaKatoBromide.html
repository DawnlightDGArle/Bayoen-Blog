Released as a part of a series of 10 bromides to promote the Puyo Puyo ON STAGE show in May 2015, this bromide of Rihona Kato depicts her dressed as Amitie, whom she played in the show. Retailing for ¥500 each and sold at the Asaka Act Theatre between May 2nd, 2015 and May 6th, 2015, this bromide could be purchased alongside other pieces of Puyo Puyo ON STAGE merchandise as well as signed by the actress herself.
