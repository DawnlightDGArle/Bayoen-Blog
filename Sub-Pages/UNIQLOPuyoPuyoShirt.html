Released on September 23rd, 2019, this shirt was released under UNIQLO's "The Game Classic Pixels" line of t-shirts alongside other SEGA game themed t-shirts and retailed for ¥1500. The shirt was available in a variety of men's sizes, with the one pictured being a small. If the shirt's design was replicated either in-game or through a chain simulator, the resulting pattern would create a 5-chain all clear.
