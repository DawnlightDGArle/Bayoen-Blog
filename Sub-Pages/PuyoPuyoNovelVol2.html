Released on December 15th, 2014 as the 2nd of a 5 volume novel series, this Puyo Puyo novel, titled "Everyone's Dreams, Coming True!?" features the Puyo Puyo cast as they go on yet another adventure, with Amitie present as the primary narrator of the novel.

Summary
On her way to school, Amitie finds a colorful box sealed with unknown magic, which she hands to person after person in an attempt to open it and find out who the real owner is. Eventually, her and Carbuncle manage to open the box by mistake, and it turns out to be a cursed artifact Schezo had retrieved from a ruin, the prison of the legendary dream mist! Now everyone who touched the box is in danger of being cursed to a never-ending sleep that manifests their dreams into reality. Amitie, Arle and Ringo need to try and wake up all the dreamers so they and Schezo can seal the mist back in the box where it belongs...
