A plush of Schezo Wegey, released by BANPRESTO in 1996 under the Puyo Puyo 33 line, this Schezo was released as part of a set of 33 different Puyo Puyo plush toys, each with their own special gimmick. Schezo's gimmick is that he comes with a yellow puyo whiteboard with a clip on the backside to attach to a spot on Schezo's boot.

Overall, the plush is well made, however, both his cape and his sword are fragile and likely to fall off, so take great care in handling this particular plush toy.
