A plush set based off of the SEGA Dreamcast spin-off title, Puyo Puyo DA!. Released sometime in 2000 as UFO Catcher prizes, this plush set contains plushes of Arle, Satan, Suketoudara and Carbuncle based on their appearances in the game. The plushes are well made and accurately capture the appearance of the characters in Puyo Puyo DA!, however, the set is hard to come by as the plushes rarely go up for sale.
