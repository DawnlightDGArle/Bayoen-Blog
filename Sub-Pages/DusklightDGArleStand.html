Released in March 2021 and manufactured by Seasonal Plants, this Dusklight Doppelganger Arle acrylic stand stands approximately 4 inches (100mm) tall and features the 6 star artwork of the character as seem in the mobile game Puyo Puyo!! Quest. Dusklight Doppelganger Arle was released as part of a set of 10 acrylic stands alongside Steam City Amitie, Steam City Sig, Steam City Lidelle, Steam City Raffina, Steam City Klug, Rozatte, Starlight Lemres, Yellow Strange Klug and Lulisia.
