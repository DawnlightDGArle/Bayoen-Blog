Released in 2021 and manufactured by Seasonal Plants, this Steam City Ringo Acrylic Clock comes with two pegs to stand the clock up on a shelf or desktop and includes a AA battery to power the clock and was released as part of a set of 9 Steam City themed clocks. The clock is approximately 190mm x 190mm and retails for ¥6,000. The clock features the artwork of Steam City Ringo ★7 from Puyo Puyo!! Quest.
