About Us

The Bayoe~n Blog is a blog dedicated to showcasing and archiving more of the nuance merchandise from the video game series, Madou Monogatari and Puyo Puyo. The blog was started on a bit of a whim, when the developer of the blog wanted a way to showcase her collection and new additions while also wanting to prove at the same time why she believed that website builders such as Wix and SquareSpace were in her opinion scams. Thus, the Bayoe~n Blog was formed. Starting off as a side hustle passion project during the developer's university years, the blog eventually grew and changed into what it is today: your one stop to learn about everything Madou and Puyo merchandise! We're proud of that history and very excited that we can be your one stop to learn so much more about the merchandising scene of these two video game series and we hope that whether you're here out of curiosity or a hardcore collector looking to scout out what's out there or even if you're someone who knows nothing about either video game series, that no matter what, you enjoy what we do and appreciate our blog for what it is: Your Local Madou Puyo Merch Archive. - The Bayoe~n Blog

Below you will find introductions for members of our blog's team. Whether these team members do back-end development for the site or even just contribute large amounts of images for the site on a regular basis, at the heart of it all is a team of very passionate and dedicated people and we'd like nothing more than to introduce some of these people to you!
DawnlightDGArle - Head Site Developer

Dawnlight is a long time Madou Monogatari and Puyo Puyo fan and an avid collector for both series. She is the blog's primary contributor and is responsible for all of the site's front and back end development as well as 98% of everything on the blog. She is currently certified in Responsive Web Design and pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science and a minor in Mathematics and is highly dedicated to her works, including this blog.
