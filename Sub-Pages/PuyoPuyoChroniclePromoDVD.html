A promotional DVD for Puyo Puyo Chronicle. Released in 2016 as a preview disc for what Puyo Puyo Chronicle could be advertised as, the disc contains 6 1/2 minutes of footage featuring two Japanese women dressed as modified designs of Arle and Ally (which would later be used in a 3DS home screen theme) talking about the different aspects of Puyo Puyo Chronicle and showing gameplay in a commercial like style.
