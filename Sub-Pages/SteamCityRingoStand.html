Released in March 2021 and manufactured by Seasonal Plants, this acrylic stand of Steam City Ringo features the artwork of the 6 star as seen in the mobile game Puyo Puyo!! Quest and stands approximately 4 inches (100mm) tall. This Steam City Ringo acrylic stand was released alongside acrylic stands of Steam City Maguro, Steam City Lemres, Steam City Risukuma, Steam City Feli, Charmy Draco, Black Sig, King of Ocean Prince, Snohime and Martial Staff Raffina.
