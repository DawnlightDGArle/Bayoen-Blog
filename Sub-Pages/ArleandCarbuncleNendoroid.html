Released in October 2021, this nendoroid of Arle and Carbuncle, produced by Good Smile Company, includes Arle and Carbuncle as well as other parts to pose Arle in a variety of different poses plus 5 Puyo accessories featuring the 5 main colours from the series. She is Nendoroid number 1582 in GSC's main Nendoroid lineup and retails for $55.99 USD.

Description from GSC US Website

"Let's go on lots of adventures together!" From the popular game "Puyo Puyo!! Quest" comes a Nendoroid of Arle & Carbuncle! Arle comes with both a standard face plate and a cute winking expression. Carbuncle and several Gumi Puyo have been brought down into adorable Nendoroid size as well. Arle can also be posed holding Carbuncle using one of her arm parts. Be sure to add her to your collection!
Sculptor: Design COCO
Specifications: Painted ABS&PVC non-scale articulated figure with stand included. Approx. 100mm (3.94 inches) in height.
Manufacturer: Good Smile Company
