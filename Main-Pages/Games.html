* - Madou Monogatari on the i-mode is in a unique position where the game released in 2005, long after COMPILE had gone bankrupt, however, the game was developed by a third party company, ALKY, and published by SEGA©, with D4 Enterprise© not even owning the rights to redistribute the title. The game was available for 6 months before becoming no longer available. As of September 2021, it was confirmed that the tecnology to preserve and emulate Madou Monogatari i-mode exists and is in the process of being tested and developed, so perhaps soon we will see this title become more accessible and playable.
