Released in June 2021 as a UFO Catcher Prize, this plush set features a set of 6 plushes including Arle, Amitie, Ringo, Hello Kitty, Pompompurin and My Melody. Here, Arle is designed after Hello Kitty, Amitie is designed after My Melody and Ringo is designed after Pompompurin, with the remaining characters in the set dressed as the Puyo Puyo characters, Hello Kitty dressed as Arle, My Melody dressed as Amitie and Pompompurin dressed as Ringo.
