Released in 2020 as a part of Rakuten's Puyo Puyo Girls collection, which includes 5 canvas pouches featuring 5 female characters from the series, this canvas pouch of Ringo features on the front side Posion Ringo ★6 and Princess Ringo ★6 while featuring Peachy Ringo ★6 and Explorer Ringo ★6 on the back side. This item could be purchased by buying into a lottery, with a 10/100 chance of winning one of the 5 canvas pouches as an A-tier prize.
