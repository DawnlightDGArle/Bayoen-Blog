* - Madou Monogatari on the i-mode is in a unique position where the game released in 2005, long after COMPILE had gone bankrupt, however, the game was developed by a third party company, ALKY, and published by SEGA©. The game was available for 6 months before becoming no longer available.

** - Puyo Puyo Quest is a free-to-play non-canon mobile entry in the Puyo Puyo series and thus should be classified as a spin-off title. 3 of the SEGA era titles, being Fever, Fever 2 and 7, are considered canon mainseries titles and other titles, such as Puyo Puyo Tetris 1/2 and Puyo Puyo Chronicle, are considered non-canon sideseries titles.
